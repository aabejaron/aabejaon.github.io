Tax Brackets

The computation follows the latest income tax schedule:

  • ₱0 – ₱250,000 → 0% tax
  • ₱250,001 – ₱400,000 → 20% of the excess over ₱250,000
  • ₱400,001 – ₱800,000 → ₱30,000 + 25% of the excess over ₱400,000
  • ₱800,001 – ₱2,000,000 → ₱130,000 + 30% of the excess over ₱800,000
  • ₱2,000,001 – ₱8,000,000 → ₱490,000 + 32% of the excess over ₱2,000,000
  • ₱8,000,001 and above → ₱2,410,000 + 35% of the excess over ₱8,000,000

This calculator provides an estimated computation for educational purposes.
