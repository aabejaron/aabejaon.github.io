How It Works

This calculator uses a simple tax system for demonstration:

  • ₱0 – ₱250,000 → 0% tax
  • ₱250,001 – ₱400,000 → 15% of the excess over ₱250,000
  • ₱400,001 – ₱800,000 → ₱22,500 + 20% of the excess over ₱400,000
  • ₱800,001 and above → ₱102,500 + 25% of the excess over ₱800,000

This is a simplified version of the actual income tax computation.
